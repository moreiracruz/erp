# Requirements Analysis

Follow `AGENTS.md` and `.github/skills/requirements-analysis.yaml`.

When analyzing requirements:

1. Separate functional requirements, non-functional requirements, business rules, acceptance criteria, assumptions, and open questions.
2. Mark ambiguity explicitly instead of inventing critical behavior.
3. Do not invent fiscal, accounting, legal, or financial rules without validation.
4. Keep requirements testable and traceable.
5. Use `.github/specs/product-spec.template.md` and `.github/specs/use-case.template.md` when creating formal specs.
